Workmanship Warranty

Impacture Technologies provides adequate workmanship warranty on all the power plants. The company will periodically inspect , maintain and repair the system free of cost in the duration of the warranty. The workmanship warranty includes the civil work, the mounting structure and all the wirings. Other than this the warrantees of individual components like the solar panels and the inverters will be as provided by the manufacture.
